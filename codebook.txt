1/ "Subject" is the identifier of the observed person. It can take 30 values between 1 and 30.

2/ "Activity" defines the activity that the user was involved in at the observation time.
It can take 6 values "WALKING", "WALKING_UPSTAIRS", "WALKING_DOWNSTAIRS", "LYING", "STANDING", "SITTING".
